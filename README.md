## 🎯 Purpose of this application 

Chapee_Chatting helps you manage your customer messages on Shopee. You can reply to buyers, handle order status questions, and track conversations in one central hub. The tool saves time and helps you provide fast support to your shoppers.

## 🚀 Getting started 

When you open the app for the first time, you must connect your Shopee account.

1. Click the Settings icon in the top right corner.
2. Select the Account tab.
3. Choose the option to Link Shopee Account.
4. Enter your Shopee seller credentials in the pop-up window.
5. Grant the app permission to access your messages.
6. Return to the main dashboard to view your current chats.

## ⚙️ Core features 

The application includes several tools to help you manage your shop.

### Message organization
The dashboard sorts your messages by order status. You can see which customers have pending questions about payment, shipping, or returns in separate tabs.

### Quick replies
You can save templates for common questions. Select a template from the list and hit send to reply to a customer in two clicks. 

### Order lookups
The app shows order details next to the chat window. When a customer asks about a delivery, you see the tracking status and the items bought without leaving the message screen.

### Search function
Use the search bar to find messages from specific buyers. Type the username or the order number to see the full chat history.

## 🛡️ Privacy and security 

This application stores your data on your computer. We do not access your account password or personal files. The app sends information only to Shopee servers to fetch your chat history. You remain in control of your data at all times.

## ❓ Frequently asked questions 

### Can I use this for multiple stores?
Yes. You can add more than one account in the settings menu. Switch between accounts by clicking the profile picture at the top of the sidebar.

### Does the app run in the background?
Yes. It stays active in your system tray so you receive arrival alerts for new messages.

### What happens if the app crashes?
Restart the application to clear minor errors. If the issue persists, reinstall the software using the steps listed above.

### Can I customize my message templates? 
Go to the Templates menu to add or delete your saved responses. You can categorize them by topic to keep your library organized.

## 🔧 Troubleshooting
